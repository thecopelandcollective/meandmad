bio

Growing up in suburban Detroit, Gavin and his sister Madison, along with friend Sam were shaped by the rich musical history of the city. Coming from a homeschooling background, they were able to focus their time on what they loved and have created a unique sound that mashes 90's pop with Detroit techno while sprinkling in a bit of blues and jazz.
